# Last Testimony Reference Playbook

This slice focuses on the courtroom mystery scenario. The goal is not to clone any reference game, but to borrow durable interaction principles that make handheld narrative games feel deliberate, tactile, and dramatic.

## Ace Attorney Trial Rhythm

Source: Nintendo manual for *Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney*
https://csassets.nintendo.com/noaext/image/private/t_KA_PDF/manual-3DS-professor-layton-vs-phoenix-wright-ace-attorney-en?_a=BATCtdAA0

- Testimony and cross-examination are separate beats: the witness first states events, then the player works through testimony line by line.
- Pressing is persistent and exploratory. It can reveal new detail, new testimony, or new evidence.
- Presenting is precise. The player must connect the current testimony statement to a relevant Court Record item.
- Wrong presentation should cost credibility or trigger a setback, not feel like a neutral button press.

Applied here:
- `Witness Stand` now has two testimony statements before the hook opens.
- The hook shows `Statement X / Y`, previous/next statement controls, `Press Statement`, and a `HOLD IT!` response.
- `Present Evidence` succeeds only when the selected statement and selected evidence both match the contradiction.

## Trauma Center Pressure

Source: Nintendo UK product page for *Trauma Center: Under the Knife*
https://www.nintendo.com/en-gb/Games/Nintendo-DS/Trauma-Center-Under-the-Knife-273476.html

- Tension comes from concrete procedure: tools, time pressure, ranked performance, and patient stakes.
- The story matters because every patient has context between procedures.

Applied direction:
- Courtroom pressure should become visible as state: judge patience, witness pressure, and evidence confidence.
- Future slices should make setbacks and recovery feel procedural, not just branching text.

## Professor Layton Puzzle Framing

Source: Nintendo UK product page for *Professor Layton and the Curious Village*
https://www.nintendo.com/en-gb/Games/Nintendo-DS/Professor-Layton-and-the-Curious-Village-272563.html

- Puzzle solving is framed by a stylish animated mystery, not isolated riddles.
- Progression can be gated by attention checks and special puzzles.

Applied direction:
- Evidence inspection is now a puzzle-framed reveal with a clear prompt, hotspot-style framing, and story reward.
- `Rain on the Inside` opens `Photo Inspection`, asking the player to mark the stain beneath the sill before the staged-window path continues.
- Future slices should add deeper hint rhythm, timing, and reconstruction ordering after the inspection step.
